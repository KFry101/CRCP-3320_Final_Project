Reading My Emotions

Reading My Emotions is a 3D data sculpture that visualizes a week's worth of the artist's emotional states. The sculpture is created by collecting daily self-reported emotional data, which is then translated into a three -dimesional graph cube. Each directional axis of the cube represents a different data point: the x-axis corresponds to the time of day, the y-axis represents the intensity of the emotion, and the z-axis indicates the duration of each emotional state. These data points acroos the cube are connected with a string whose color represents the type of emotion. The resulting form is a tangled web of emotional experiences, with peaks and valleys that reflect the highs and lows of the artist's week. This piece invites the viewer to read and interpret the different strings of emotion to tell a story about the artist's emotional experience.
