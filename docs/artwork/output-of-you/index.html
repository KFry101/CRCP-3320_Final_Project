Output of You

The Output of You is a reactive mirror, displayed on a screen, that uses a live camera feed to capture and reinterpret a human’s presence. As a person interacts with this project, they will see themselves on screen surrounded by an aura, made of small particles, that shifts in response to movement and facial expression. This piece was created using TouchDesigner, the MediaPipe add-on, and a camera. Pose and face tracking drives the system, mapping movement to particle motion and facial expression to color. Through these inputs, the body becomes a controller, manifesting one’s energy into a dynamic visual. Influenced by the silent parts of human experience, this work addresses how emotion and presence are often unseen yet play a vital role in everyday interaction. The Output of You reflects emotional energy back to the viewer, blurring the boundary between how we feel and how we are seen.
