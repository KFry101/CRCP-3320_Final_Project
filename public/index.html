HackSMU VI: EZPPTX

2024

Won 1st place Overall and MLH - Most Creative Adobe Express Add-On EzPPTX solves an issue within Adobe Express.

Express does not give users the ability to turn its presentations into PowerPoint files, only image files (PNG, JPEG, PDF). You would have to use a different Adobe software, Adobe Acrobat, to convert PDF to PowerPoint. EzPPTX is an Adobe Express add-on that consists of two features: conversion to a .pptx file and conversion to a .docx file.
